About Our Project

This Project is a smart community recycling bin that keeps the area cleaned by moving around by its own self and then recycles the waste that it had cleaned. It serves as a factor that could help for reaching the goals of SDG 11, it helps in creating jobs opportunities in recycling, education, and waste management, supporting also long-term economic health in our world, it helps in acknowledging and preventing major global issues like climate change and pollution.

Our autonomous recycling bin uses advanced sensors, AI technology, and efficient sorting mechanisms to identify, collect, and properly sort waste materials. By operating autonomously, our solution reduces manual labor while maximizing recycling efficiency.
